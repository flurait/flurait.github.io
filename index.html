- Why you should choose our fleet?
Our fleet of state-of-the-art private jets is equipped with the latest technology and amenities to ensure that your journey is as smooth and enjoyable as possible. Whether you're traveling for business or pleasure, we guarantee that you'll arrive at your destination feeling relaxed and refreshed.
- How does the company ensure flight safety?
We adhere to the highest safety standards and only work with experienced and professional pilots. All our aircraft are regularly checked and maintained.
- What is the rental procedure and what documents are required?
To rent an aircraft or helicopter, you need to contact our manager, who will help you choose the appropriate type of aircraft and complete all the necessary documents. Typically, a passport and a valid driver's or pilot's license are required to rent.
- What certificates and licenses does the company have?
Our company has all the necessary certificates and licenses to carry out activities in the field of aircraft and helicopter rental. We also operate in accordance with international safety standards.
